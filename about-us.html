About Our Department

Morrisville Fire Department proudly protects 7500 year round residents in a 42.5 square mile district. We operate out of 1 station that protects a primarily rural area. Our department is a public department whose members are on a volunteer status. Morrisville Fire Department is located in the center of New York State. In our Fire District is the Morrisville State College. Morrisville Fire Department has 35 members. Plus several college students who provide mutual aid while attending college. Some of the students are members in their hometowns and ride under a "Mutual aid Agreement" however some students have chosen to join the Morrisville Fire Department outright.
